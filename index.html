Who is Catherine?

In her last life...

She was an artist, a teacher, an traveller and a parent to three beautiful girls. Now her children are adults and Catherine has embarked on a journey of spiritual exploration and growth.

In her current life...

She is a lightworker, a blueprint designer, an artist, a researcher, a lifelong learner, and a fur-mom to her beautiful poodle-unicorn, Abby. Certified in Soul Realignment and currently studying Quantum Flow, Catherine is a truth-seeker on a quest for knowledge to help empower and inform others.
